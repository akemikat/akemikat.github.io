About

Software that used to be out of reach

I started Red Eagle Tech because I kept seeing the same problem: small and medium businesses needed proper, tailored software but couldn't justify the budgets that large enterprises take for granted. So we set out to change that. We use agentic coding tools in our own workflows now, which has made a real difference to what we can deliver and how quickly.

Before software, I worked across marketing, operations, education, healthcare, and business development. That mix means I've sat on the other side of the table. I know what it's like to need a technical solution and not know where to start. I got into engineering to fix my own problems, then teamed up with others who wanted to do the same for businesses.

We're based in London and work with local businesses and SMEs across the UK, building bespoke software that fits what they actually need.
