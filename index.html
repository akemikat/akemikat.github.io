Our Products

MeldEagle is our Shopify automation platform, available on the Azure Marketplace. It's built for e-commerce businesses that need to manage product listings and orders at scale without the manual overhead.

CDR API is our Content Disarm and Reconstruct API, designed for businesses that need to sanitise uploaded files and documents before they reach internal systems. It strips potentially malicious content from files while preserving the original format, and is available on a pay-per-use basis.
